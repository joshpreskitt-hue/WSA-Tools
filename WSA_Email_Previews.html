Email 4 — Work Order Closure

The green-themed closure email fires when you set Status to "Completed." Fill in Resolution Notes (col O) before changing the status — those notes appear in this email.

From:WSA Operations <your.email@waynestem.org>
To:T. Williams <t.williams@waynestem.org>
Sub:Work Order Resolved — WO-26-001 | Wayne STEM Academy
Wayne STEM Academy — Operations
Work Order Resolved ✓
Completed on April 3, 2026

Hi T. Williams,

Your work order has been resolved. Here is a summary of what was completed:

WO-26-001
StatusCompleted
CampusWSA Main Campus
CategoryHVAC
Room / AreaRoom 204
Resolved byJP
Original issueAC unit not cooling — classroom temperature is over 80°F
Resolution notesReplaced air filter and recharged refrigerant. Unit tested at 68°F output. Should hold through summer.

If the issue returns or you have a new concern, please submit a new work order.

This is an automated message from the WSA Work Order System. Do not reply to this email.
For urgent issues, contact the Operations Office directly.

Best practice: Always fill col O (Resolution Notes) before changing status to Completed. A one-sentence description of what was done closes the loop professionally and reduces "did anyone fix that?" follow-ups.
