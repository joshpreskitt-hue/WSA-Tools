Transportation Department Safe, on-time, dignified transportation is often the first and last experience students have with WSA each day — your role shapes that experience profoundly.
WSA Mission & Vision
Every child deserves to arrive safely and leave feeling cared for. Transportation staff are trusted ambassadors of WSA's culture — maintaining safety, building relationships, and representing our commitment to excellence from the first mile to the last.

Vehicle Safety & Operations
Compliance, condition, and safe operating practices
Pre/Post-Trip Vehicle Inspections
Completes required pre- and post-trip inspection checklists; documents defects and reports them through proper channels before operating vehicle.
Needs Development
Inspections are skipped, incomplete, or defects go unreported; requires prompting.
Meets Expectations
Consistently completes all inspections; reports issues before operation.
Exceeds Expectations
Zero missed inspections; maintains documentation above standard; mentors others.
Adherence to Traffic Laws & CDL Standards
Operates vehicle in full compliance with NC traffic laws, DOT regulations, and CDL requirements at all times.
Needs Development
Moving violations, citation, or documented unsafe driving behaviors.
Meets Expectations
Zero violations; follows all posted rules and district policies.
Exceeds Expectations
Exemplary record; voluntarily pursues additional safety training; zero incidents.
Route Management & Timeliness
Maintains assigned route schedule; communicates proactively about delays; adapts appropriately to route changes or emergencies.
Needs Development
Chronic delays, missed stops, or poor communication about route disruptions.
Meets Expectations
On schedule; communicates appropriately; adapts calmly to disruptions.
Exceeds Expectations
Consistently early; proactive communication; suggests route efficiency improvements.

Student Management & Positive Culture
Creating a safe, dignified environment for every rider
Student Safety Procedures (Loading/Unloading)
Follows all loading/unloading procedures; accounts for all students; confirms safe boarding and exiting before departing.
Needs Development
Procedures not consistently followed; loading/unloading errors documented.
Meets Expectations
Full procedure compliance; zero incidents; students accounted for.
Exceeds Expectations
Models perfect procedure; trains new staff; zero deviations across the year.
Student Behavior Management
Maintains appropriate student behavior on the vehicle using positive, consistent strategies aligned with WSA's values and conduct expectations.
Needs Development
Frequent unresolved behavior incidents; management strategies ineffective or inappropriate.
Meets Expectations
Consistent, appropriate management; documents and reports incidents correctly.
Exceeds Expectations
Proactively builds rapport; incidents rare; celebrates positive behavior; positive bus culture.
Relationship with Students & Families
Communicates respectfully and professionally with students and their families; represents WSA's mission in community interactions.
Needs Development
Family complaints documented; communication is inconsistent or inappropriate.
Meets Expectations
Professional, respectful interactions; families feel welcomed and informed.
Exceeds Expectations
Families request this driver; known as a positive, trusted community presence.

WSA Core Values in Action
Living WSA's mission beyond the vehicle
Dependability & Attendance
Reports on time consistently; absence rate within acceptable range; provides advance notice and arranges coverage appropriately.
Needs Development
Chronic tardiness or absences cause route disruptions; notification procedures not followed.
Meets Expectations
Reliable attendance; provides proper notice; minimal disruption to operations.
Exceeds Expectations
Zero unplanned absences; volunteers for coverage; teammates rely on this person.
Transparency & Communication
Communicates route issues, student incidents, and vehicle concerns to supervisors promptly and accurately.
Needs Development
Issues go unreported; communication is delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate.
Meets Expectations
Communicates timely, accurately; keeps supervisor informed of all relevant issues.
Exceeds Expectations
Proactive communication; anticipates issues; documentation is exemplary.
Growth Mindset & Professionalism
Accepts feedback positively, pursues ongoing training, and maintains professionalism in all school and community interactions.
Needs Development
Resistant to feedback or training; unprofessional conduct documented.
Meets Expectations
Receptive to coaching; completes required training; professional at all times.
Exceeds Expectations
Actively seeks development; visible growth; represents WSA with distinction.
