Starting today, we are launching a centralized work order system for reporting and tracking all facilities, maintenance, and IT issues across both campuses. This replaces verbal reports, texts, and informal emails — and means every request gets logged, tracked, and followed up on automatically.

Here is what you need to know.

How to Submit a Work Order

1
Scan the QR code posted in your break room, front office, or custodial area — or click the link below. Takes less than 2 minutes from your phone.
2
Fill out the short form. Tell us your name, email, campus, what kind of issue it is, where it is, how urgent it is, and describe the problem. You can also attach a photo.
3
You're done. You'll receive an email confirmation with your work order number within seconds. No follow-up needed on your end.

What happens after you submit

📧
Instant confirmation
You'll receive an email with your work order number and a summary of what you submitted.
🔔
Status updates
You'll be notified by email whenever your work order status changes — no need to check in with anyone.
✅
Closure notification
When your issue is resolved, you'll receive a final email with a summary of what was done.
📊
Live dashboard
You can check the status of any work order — yours or anyone else's — on the team dashboard.

How to choose the right priority

🔴 Urgent

Safety hazard, water leak, no power, broken lock, or anything that requires immediate attention today.

🟠 High

Significantly impacts teaching or learning and should be addressed within 1–2 days.

🔵 Normal

A real issue that should be fixed but doesn't prevent normal operations. Within the week is fine.

⚪ Low

Minor cosmetic or convenience issue with no real urgency. When time allows.

Track any work order on the live team dashboard — viewable from any device, no login required.
