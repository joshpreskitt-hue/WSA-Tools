To print with full color:

  1. Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac) to open Print dialog
  2. Click "More settings" or "Advanced"
  3. Check the box: "Background graphics" or "Print backgrounds"
  4. Set paper size to Letter, margins to None
  5. Click Print
